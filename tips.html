Money Tips for Youngins

  • 💸 Track your spending weekly — know where every rand goes.
  • 🛍️ Set a monthly budget for food, transport, and fun.
  • 💰 Save at least 10% of your income — even if it’s small.
  • 📱 Use virtual cards for subscriptions to get discounts.
  • 🚀 Avoid impulse buys — wait 24 hours before buying anything non-essential.
  • 🧾 Keep receipts — it helps with tracking and tax purposes.
  • 💳 Only use credit if you can pay it off immediately.
  • 📈 Invest in small ways — even R50 a week adds up over time.
